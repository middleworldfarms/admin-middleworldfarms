HOW IT WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Create Your Succession Plan
   • Select crop, variety, harvest window
   • Click "Calculate Harvest Window"
   • Plan appears in table on left side

STEP 2: Click "Analyze Plan" Button
   • Blue button with 🧠 brain icon
   • Sends plan to AI with RAG-powered context
   • AI uses 76 knowledge entries for analysis

STEP 3: AI Response Appears Below Buttons
   • Response area becomes visible
   • Shows AI's analysis and recommendations
   • Detects actionable items:
     ❌ Remove succession X
     📅 Delay/Advance succession X by Y days/weeks
     📏 Spacing concerns (flagged for review)
     🌿 Companion planting suggestions

STEP 4: Accept Button Appears (if recommendations detected)
   • Only shows when AI suggests succession-related changes
   • Green "Accept Recommendations" button
   • Grey "Request Modifications" button for iteration

STEP 5: Click "Accept Recommendations"
   • Modal popup shows detected changes:
     ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
     │  ✓ Accept AI Recommendations                        │

     │                                                      │
     │  [Cancel] [Accept & Apply Changes]                  │
     └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STEP 6: Confirm and Watch Magic Happen! ✨
   • Plan automatically updates:
     ✓ Succession 3 dates shift by +7 days
     ✓ Seeding: Apr 10 → Apr 17
     ✓ Transplant: Apr 25 → May 2
     ✓ Harvest: Jun 10-25 → Jun 17-Jul 2
   • Table flashes green to show update

   • Full audit trail logged

1. Remove Successions
   AI says: "Remove succession 3"
   Result: Succession 3 deleted, remaining renumbered (4→3, 5→4)

2. Delay Timing
   AI says: "Delay succession 2 by 1 week"
   Result: All dates for succession 2 shift +7 days

3. Advance Timing
   AI says: "Advance succession 4 by 2 weeks"
   Result: All dates for succession 4 shift -14 days

4. Generic Timing (defaults to 7 days)
   AI says: "Succession 3 is too early"
   Result: All dates for succession 3 shift +7 days

⚠️ FLAGGED FOR REVIEW (shows in modal, you decide):

5. Spacing Concerns
   AI says: "Spacing in succession 2 seems tight"
   Result: Shows in modal, prompts you to review manually

6. Companion Planting
   AI says: "Consider adding nasturtiums"
   Result: Shows in modal, informational only

EXAMPLE WORKFLOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. You create a 5-succession Brussels Sprouts plan
   Apr 1 → Apr 15 → May 1 → May 15 → Jun 1

2. Click "🧠 Analyze Plan"

3. AI analyzes (using RAG knowledge):
   ✓ Checks companion planting entries (nasturtiums good!)
   ✓ Checks rotation rules (follows legumes well)
   ✓ Checks UK calendar (April = ideal for brassicas)
   ✓ Analyzes timing gaps

4. AI responds:
   "Your plan follows good rotation principles! Brussels Sprouts after
   legumes is excellent. However, successions 1-2 are only 14 days apart.
   I recommend delaying succession 2 by 1 week to allow better spacing.
   Consider companion planting nasturtiums (repel aphids)."

5. Accept button appears below response

6. You click "✓ Accept Recommendations"

7. Modal shows:
   📅 Delay succession 2 by 1 week
   🌿 Companion: nasturtiums (informational)

8. You click "Accept & Apply Changes"

9. Plan updates:
   Succession 2: Apr 15 → Apr 22 (all dates shift +7 days)
   Table flashes green
   Success: "1 change(s) applied to your succession plan"

10. Ready to submit to FarmOS! 🎉
