✅ Shows visual proportions of each phase
   • Seeding phase (green)
   • Transplant/growing phase (cyan)
   • Harvest window (yellow)

✅ Displays day counts in each segment
   • "35d" means 35 days in that phase
   • Total days shown on right

✅ Emoji indicators for clarity
   • 🌱 Seed
   • 🌿 Transplant
   • 🌾 Harvest

✅ Responsive to crop type
   • Direct sown = 2 segments (seed → harvest)
   • Transplanted = 3 segments (seed → transplant → harvest)

✅ Color-coded gradients
   • Green gradient = Growing/seeding phase
   • Cyan gradient = Transplant phase
   • Yellow gradient = Harvest window

HOW IT CALCULATES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FOR TRANSPLANTED CROPS:

Total Days = Harvest End - Seeding Date
           = Oct 30 - Aug 1 = 115 days

Phase 1 (GREEN): Seeding → Transplant
           = Sept 5 - Aug 1 = 35 days
           = 35/115 = 30.4% of timeline

Phase 2 (CYAN): Transplant → Harvest Start
           = Sept 15 - Sept 5 = 45 days
           = 45/115 = 39.1% of timeline

Phase 3 (YELLOW): Harvest Window
           = Oct 30 - Sept 15 = 35 days
           = 35/115 = 30.4% of timeline

FOR DIRECT SOWN CROPS:

Total Days = Harvest End - Seeding Date
           = Jun 30 - Apr 15 = 75 days

Phase 1 (GREEN): Seeding → Harvest Start
           = Jun 15 - Apr 15 = 60 days
           = 60/75 = 80% of timeline

Phase 2 (YELLOW): Harvest Window
           = Jun 30 - Jun 15 = 15 days
           = 15/75 = 20% of timeline
